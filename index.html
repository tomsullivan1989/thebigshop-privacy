The Big Shop — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 March 2026

Overview

The Big Shop ("we", "our", "the app") is an AI-powered shopping and recipe assistant. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we store your email address and display name to identify your account. If you use the app without signing in, we create an anonymous account with no personal information.
  • Recipes, shopping lists, and pantry items: Content you create in the app is stored in Google Cloud Firestore so it syncs across your devices.
  • User preferences: Household size, dietary restrictions, allergies, cuisine preferences, and preferred units — used to personalise AI suggestions.
  • Voice recordings: When you use the voice input feature, audio is sent to our server for transcription and immediately discarded after processing. We do not store audio recordings.
  • Photos: When you use the camera to scan recipes or pantry items, images are sent to our server for AI processing and immediately discarded. We do not store your photos.
  • Usage data: We track the number of AI requests per month for quota purposes. We do not track which features you use or how often you open the app.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the core app functionality: saving recipes, managing shopping lists and pantry items
  • To process voice and image inputs via AI (Google Gemini) for recipe parsing and suggestions
  • To personalise AI responses based on your dietary preferences
  • To enforce usage quotas (free vs. paid tiers)

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, database (Firestore), crash reporting (Crashlytics)
  • Google Gemini AI: Recipe parsing, ingredient categorisation, recipe suggestions
  • Google Cloud Run: Backend hosting
  • Google Play Billing: Subscription management

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore with encryption at rest and in transit. All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS. We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties beyond the services listed above.

Data Retention

  • Account data and app content: Retained as long as your account exists
  • Voice recordings and photos: Not retained — processed and immediately discarded
  • Usage quota data: Automatically deleted after 90 days

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your data: Delete individual recipes, shopping list items, or pantry items at any time within the app
  • Delete your account: Contact us at the email below and we will delete all data associated with your account
  • Export your data: Contact us to request a copy of your data

Children's Privacy

The Big Shop is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
